Baseball player's size effects on home runs

For this project I decided to use a dataset of baseball players that includes the players' weight, height, handedness, and home run totals. I thought it would be interesting to look at the effect a player's size had on the amount of home runs that player hit.

Weight and Home Runs

This first section is the players' weight plotted out against their home run totals. Where I had initially thought that a players weight would have a large impact on the amount of home runs a player hit, there appears to be only a very very small correlation, if any at all.

Height and Home Runs

I decided to explore height next, but it seemed to have even less of an effect on a players home run totals.
